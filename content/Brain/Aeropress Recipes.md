# Quick and Dirty

- [[Aeropress#Inverted|Aeropress > Inverted]]
- [[Coffee Beans|Coffee Beans]] | 15 g | ground
	- around 24 clicks on a [[Comandante C40 MK4|Comandante C40 MK4]]
- [[Brew Water Recipe#Brew Water|Brew Water Recipe > Brew Water]] | 220 ml

---

1. **Boil** a kettle of water. During that time, **grind** your coffee, prepare the Aeropress, etc. for the *water to cool down slightly*
2. Dump in **all the brew water**, slowing down if you notice too much foam
3. **Stir for 1 min** continuously
4. **Wait for 1 min**
5. **Press**

# Maldaner (Light Roast)

- [[Aeropress#Inverted|Aeropress > Inverted]]
- [[Coffee Beans|Coffee Beans]] | 15 g | ground
	- around 24 clicks on a [[Comandante C40 MK4|Comandante C40 MK4]]
- [[Brew Water Recipe#Brew Water|Brew Water Recipe > Brew Water]] | 220 ml

---

1. **Boil** a kettle of water. During that time, **grind** your coffee, prepare the Aeropress, etc. for the *water to cool down slightly*
2. **Bloom** with **50g** of water for **25s**
3. Dump in **all the remaining brew water** (total 240g), slowing down if you notice too much foam
4. **Wait for 2 min**
5. **Press**
