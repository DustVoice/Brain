What would a Zettelkasten be without Zettel?

That's right, a Kasten!

And although, as a German, I wouldn't say no to a Kasten, the absence of Zettel would defeat the purpose of this whole endeavour.

Keep in mind that the majority of my _Zettel_ live in the root of my Brain!

The only ones contained within this subdirectory are Notes that have no title.
They replace a Daily Journal and are mainly meant to be discoverable through Backlinks.

Any note that _could_ have a title, serves a minimally greater purpose and therefore _should_ have a title, lives in the main Brain.
