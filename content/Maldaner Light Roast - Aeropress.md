## Ingredients

- [[Aeropress#Inverted]]
- 15 g [[Coffee Beans]], ground
	- around 24 clicks on a [[Comandante C40 MK4]]
- 220 ml [[Brew Water Recipe#Brew Water]]

## Directions

1. Boil a kettle of **water**. During that time, grind your **coffee**, prepare the Aeropress, etc. for the water to cool down slightly
2. Bloom with **50g of water** for ==25 seconds==
3. Dump in all the **remaining brew water** (total 240g), slowing down if you notice too much foam
4. Wait for ==2 min==
5. Press
