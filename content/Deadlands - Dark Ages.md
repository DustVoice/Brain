[Deadlands: Dark Ages](https://peginc.com/product/deadlands-dark-ages/) is set in medieval Britain.

Following world and history altering consequences from the 1884's Weird West, _Morgan le Fay_ rises again, but this time without _Merlin_ anywhere to be seen.

Magic isn't abundant, some say even a fairy tale and living conditions are miserable.
The best cure for diseases seems to be blood-letting.

So how the heck did this weird metal ... thing appear seemingly out of nowhere?
