> [!cite]
> Mergiraf can solve [a wide range of Git merge conflicts](https://mergiraf.org/conflicts.html). That's because it's aware of the trees in your files! Thanks to [its understanding of your language](https://mergiraf.org/languages.html), it can often reconcile the needs of both sides.

## Install

### [[Cargo]]

```sh
cargo install --locked mergiraf
```

### [[Cargo#binstall]]

```sh
cargo binstall mergiraf
```
