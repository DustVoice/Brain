These are my _Journal Entries_.
They are composed of
- [[Journal/Daily/\|Daily Notes]]
- [[Journal/Weekly/\|Weekly Notes]]
- [[Journal/Monthly/\|Monthly Notes]]
- [[Journal/Yearly/\|Yearly Notes]]

Note that these notes are rarely populated by hand!
As I use this Obsidian Vault for both public **and** private data, having a bog standard _bullet list_ in my daily notes, for example, means that I could leak private information, as the whole bullet list would get published.

The way I do it is by using/creating _Unique Notes_ or [[Zettel/]] and then listing every _Zettel of the Day_ within the Daily Note dynamically.
This way I can select which Zettel to actually publish!
