A [[Virtual Tabletop]], developed by _SmiteWorks USA LLC_, for a huge amount of game systems, with a metric fckton of (official!) content available in either their [store](https://www.fantasygrounds.com/store/) or even on [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/franchise/SmiteWorks).

Once you get familiar with the way FG does things and maybe get over the opinionated UI, it's very versatile and powerful both in automation and manual intervention.

I found it to be _maybe_ a bit lacking in final polish and ease of preperation, when compared to [[Foundry VTT]], but what it lacks it more than makes up in the ability to **spontaneously** correct something, intervene or even make up on the spot.
The draggable dice (or better draggable **everything**) is always a player favorite, together with easy on-the-fly automation using (pretty easy to learn) effect syntax in the combat tracker for the Game Master!
