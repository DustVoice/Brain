The _Deadlands_ settings line (you could almost say genre), is one of Pinnacle's and therefore [[Savage Worlds]]' absolute staples!

While the original [[Deadlands - the Weird West]] focuses on a good ol' western theme, the other variants like [[Deadlands - Hell on Earth]], [[Deadlands - Lost Colony]] and more focus on completely different aspects, genres and vibes.
